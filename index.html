A hackathon is a collaborative event where programmers, designers, and other tech enthusiasts come together to build innovative solutions within a limited timeframe, typically 24-48 hours. Participants work in teams to solve challenges, create projects, and compete for prizes.

Hackathons are for everyone! There are events appropriate for all skill levels, from beginners to experienced professionals. Many hackathons specifically welcome newcomers and provide mentors to help you along the way.

Once you've created an account on HackConnect, navigate to the Mentors section where you can browse available mentors, view their expertise and schedule, and book a session that fits your needs. Some sessions may be free, while others may require HackConnect credits.
