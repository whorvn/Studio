Overview

Join us for a 17-day virtual hackathon focused on building innovative solutions for the future of finance.

The Global FinTech Challenge invites developers, designers, and entrepreneurs to tackle pressing challenges in the financial technology space. From building more inclusive banking solutions to creating secure blockchain applications, this hackathon offers a platform to showcase your skills and potentially transform the industry.

Participants will have access to exclusive APIs from leading financial institutions, mentorship from industry experts, and opportunities to network with venture capital firms looking to invest in promising FinTech solutions.

Challenge Themes

Security & Fraud Prevention

Develop solutions to enhance security in financial transactions and prevent fraud in digital banking.

Financial Inclusion

Create tools that make financial services accessible to underbanked and underserved populations.

Wealth Management

Build innovative platforms for investment management, financial planning, and wealth building.

Blockchain & Crypto

Develop decentralized applications or infrastructure for digital assets and blockchain technology.

Tech Stack

Participants are free to use any technology stack they prefer. However, we'll provide special support and resources for projects using:

Rules

  • Eligibility

    Open to individual participants worldwide who are at least 18 years of age. Participants can form teams of 2-4 members.

  • Project Requirements

    All code must be written during the hackathon period. You may use open-source libraries and frameworks, but the core solution must be original.

  • Intellectual Property

    Teams retain ownership of their projects. By participating, you grant the organizers a non-exclusive license to showcase your project for promotional purposes.

  • Submissions

    Projects must be submitted through the hackathon platform by August 10, 2023, at 11:59 PM UTC. Late submissions will not be accepted.

  • Judging Criteria

    Projects will be evaluated based on innovation (25%), technical complexity (25%), business potential (25%), and presentation quality (25%).

  • Code of Conduct

    All participants are expected to adhere to our Code of Conduct. Any violations may result in disqualification.
