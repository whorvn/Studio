About the Hackathon

Join us for the AI Innovation Challenge, where developers, designers, and entrepreneurs come together to build cutting-edge AI applications that address real-world challenges. This hackathon focuses on creating ethical and accessible AI solutions that can make a positive impact across industries.

Participants will have access to exclusive APIs, mentorship from industry experts, and workshops to enhance their skills. Whether you're an AI veteran or just starting out, this hackathon offers a platform to learn, network, and showcase your creativity.

The event will be conducted in a hybrid format, allowing both virtual participation and an in-person experience in San Francisco for those who can attend.

Challenge Tracks

AI for Healthcare
Intermediate

Develop AI solutions that improve healthcare delivery, diagnosis, or patient care. Focus areas include predictive analytics, medical imaging analysis, and personalized medicine applications.

Climate Tech
Advanced

Build AI-powered tools to address climate challenges, such as carbon footprint monitoring, renewable energy optimization, or environmental conservation solutions.

Accessibility
Beginner

Create AI applications that improve digital accessibility for people with disabilities, including voice interfaces, automated captioning, or assistive technologies.

EdTech
Intermediate

Develop AI solutions for education, such as personalized learning platforms, intelligent tutoring systems, or tools that help educators enhance their teaching methods.
