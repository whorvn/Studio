Financial Innovation Challenge

Reimagining the future of financial services through technology

June 15, 2025 - June 18, 2025
Virtual Event
Max 200 Participants

About the Hackathon

Join us for an exciting three-day virtual hackathon focused on innovation in financial services. The Financial Innovation Challenge brings together developers, designers, and financial experts to create cutting-edge solutions addressing real-world challenges in the banking and finance sector.

Whether you're interested in blockchain, AI-powered financial tools, or reimagining the customer experience, this hackathon provides the perfect opportunity to showcase your skills and compete for prizes.

Eligibility & Requirements
  • Open to developers, designers, business analysts, and finance professionals
  • Participants must be 18 years or older
  • Teams of 2-5 members required
  • All experience levels welcome
  • Participants must have their own computer and internet access
  • No geographic restrictions

Challenge Tracks

Payment Innovation
Intermediate

Create next-generation payment solutions that make transactions faster, more secure, and more accessible for all users. Consider exploring contactless payments, biometric authorization, or cross-border payment optimizations.

Security & Fraud Prevention
Advanced

Develop innovative solutions to protect financial data, prevent fraud, and enhance cybersecurity in banking applications. Consider AI-based fraud detection, secure authentication methods, or blockchain-based verification systems.

Financial Inclusion
Beginner

Build solutions that make financial services more accessible to underserved populations. Consider simplified banking interfaces, educational tools, microfinance solutions, or services for the unbanked.

Judging Criteria

Innovation (25%)

Originality and creativity of the solution; addresses the problem in a novel way.

Technical Implementation (25%)

Quality of code, architecture choices, and technical difficulty of implementation.

Impact & Feasibility (25%)

Potential for real-world impact and practical implementation; scalability and sustainability.

User Experience (25%)

Usability, design quality, and overall user experience of the solution.
